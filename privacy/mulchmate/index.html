Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

Mulch Mate ("the App") is designed to help users calculate mulch needs for their garden beds. Your privacy is important, and this Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No account or login is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No data is transmitted to any server

2. Local Data Storage

The App stores data locally on your device using secure local storage (Hive database).

This may include:

  • Garden bed configurations
  • Bag size and price settings
  • Language preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect user data, including:

  • No analytics tools
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking technologies

4. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for all users, including children, as it does not collect any personal data.

5. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data leakage from external servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
