Flora Mate Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Flora Mate is a simple offline garden plant app developed by TerraMate Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

1. Data Collection

Flora Mate does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with us. The app does not require user registration or a user account.

2. Offline Use

Flora Mate is designed to work offline. Plant data, favorites, notes, and user-added plants are stored locally on your device.

3. Photos

If you add photos to your own plants, those photos remain stored locally on your device. Flora Mate does not upload your photos to any server.

4. Internet Access

Flora Mate may open external websites when you choose to follow a link, for example plant reference pages, Google Play, PayPal, Ko-fi, or email feedback. These external services may have their own privacy policies.

5. Payments and Donations

If you choose to support the app through PayPal or Ko-fi, the payment is handled by the selected external provider. Flora Mate does not process or store payment details.

6. Advertising

Flora Mate does not display third-party ads.

7. Children

Flora Mate is not specifically directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page.
